1. HOW POINTS (XP / REPUTATION) ARE EARNED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  ACTIVITY                             XP EARNED   NOTES
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Submit a daily writing answer        +10 XP      Once per question.
                                                   Awarded on first
                                                   submission only.

  Post a question in Community         +2 XP       Once per question.

  Answer a question in Community       +5 XP       Awarded on submit.

  Receive an upvote on your answer     +10 XP      Awarded when someone
    (community or daily answer)                    upvotes your content.
                                                   Reversed if upvote
                                                   is removed (-10 XP).

  Your community answer is marked      +25 XP      Awarded when a
    "Best Answer" by question owner               moderator/admin marks
                                                   your answer as best.

  Your answer is marked "Doubt         +15 XP      Awarded when a
    Solved"                                        moderator marks your
                                                   community answer as
                                                   solving the doubt.

  Complete a Silent Library session    +1 XP/min   Awarded on session
    (study session)                               end. 30 min = 30 XP.
                                                   No cap per session.

  Complete a Prelims Mock Test         +1 to       Formula:
    (MCQ scoring)                      +50 XP      round(percentage / 2)
                                                   e.g. 80% → 40 XP
                                                        50% → 25 XP
                                                        0%  →  1 XP
                                                   Minimum always 1 XP.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  NOTE: Mains (Subjective) mock tests do NOT award XP —
        they are practice-only with no MCQ scoring.
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

2. XP ACCUMULATION → RANKS (THE LADDER)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  XP THRESHOLD   LEVEL NAME    RANK TITLE        COSMETICS UNLOCKED
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  0+             Beginner      Aspirant           Inkwell theme
  50+            Learner       Cadet              Saffron theme
  200+           Contributor   Strategist         Mission theme
  500+           Scholar       Officer            Cloister theme
  1000+          Mentor        Senior Officer     Cabinet theme
                                                  Service theme

  • Rank up is automatic — no action needed.
  • The rank title appears next to your name across the UI.
  • Cosmetic themes (dashboard colour skins) unlock at each rank.
    None are sold; all are earned through study.

  Role auto-promotion:
    At 200 XP (Contributor level), your account role is silently
    upgraded from USER → CONTRIBUTOR. This allows you to:
    • Mark your own questions as "solved"
    • Additional moderation features (future)


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
3. STREAK SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  • A "study day" is counted when you complete at least one Silent
    Library session on that calendar day (IST).
  • Consecutive study days increment your streak counter.
  • Missing a day resets the streak to 1 (not 0) on the next session.
  • Streak is displayed on your profile and the Rewards page.
  • The streak does NOT directly award XP — it unlocks the Streak 30
    badge (see Section 4).


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
4. BADGES (TROPHY ROOM)  — 6 total
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  BADGE         RARITY      HOW TO EARN
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  First Blood   COMMON      Submit your first answer — either a daily
                            writing answer OR a community answer.
                            Progress: earned/not earned.

  Night Owl     RARE        Complete at least one Silent Library
                            session (any duration).
                            Progress: earned/not earned.

  Streak 30     EPIC        Maintain a 30-day consecutive study
                            streak (30 days of Silent Library sessions,
                            one per day, no gap).
                            Progress shown as: X/30 days.

  Flawless      LEGENDARY   Score 100% on a mock test that has
                            at least 50 questions (Prelims MCQ only).
                            Progress: 0/1.

  Scholar       EPIC        Accumulate 500+ XP total.
                            Progress shown as: X/500 XP.

  Cabinet       MYTHIC      Accumulate 1000+ XP total (reach the
                            "Mentor / Senior Officer" rank).
                            Progress shown as: X/1000 XP.

  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  Badges are computed live from your actual activity data.
  There is no separate "badges" table — they reflect your real stats.


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
5. LEADERBOARDS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  • Reputation Leaderboard  — total XP, all-time, shown on Rewards page.
  • Study Time (Daily)      — minutes studied today.
  • Study Time (Weekly)     — minutes studied this week.
  • Study Time (All-time)   — total minutes ever studied.

  Leaderboards are backed by Redis sorted sets.
  On a full content wipe (wipe_content.py --confirm), all leaderboard
  data is cleared alongside user reputations.


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
6. QUICK REFERENCE — MAX EARNABLE XP PER SESSION TYPE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  Silent Library (1 hour)     → 60 XP
  Mock test (100% accuracy)   → 50 XP
  Daily answer + upvotes      → 10 XP (submit) + 10/upvote received
  Post + answer community Q   → 2 XP (post) + 5 XP (answer)
  Best answer marked          → 25 XP (one-time, per answer)

  Path to Mentor (1000 XP) rough example
  ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    10 daily answers            → 100 XP
    50 mins/day × 14 days       → 700 XP
    3 mock tests at 80%         →  120 XP
    ─────────────────────────────
    Total                       → ~920 XP  (in ~2 weeks of real usage)


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
7. WHAT DOES NOT EARN POINTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  • Reading current affairs (no XP)
  • Browsing PYQs (no XP)
  • Logging in / visiting pages (no XP)
  • Receiving a downvote (no XP change — downvotes only affect
    the answer's upvote count display, not reputation)
  • Mains subjective mock tests (no XP, practice only)


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
8. IMPORTANT NOTES FOR ADMIN
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  • Running `python wipe_content.py --confirm` now also resets
    all user XP (reputation) and streak_days to 0, and clears
    silent_sessions. This ensures leaderboard + badges start clean.

  • The `--keep-pyq` flag preserves past_year_problems during a wipe.

  • To see a user's full XP history:
      GET /api/v1/users/me/reputation/history
    Returns last 20 reputation log entries with reason codes.

  • Reason codes in logs:
      "answer"            community answer submitted
      "question"          community question posted
      "upvote"            upvote received on content
      "upvote_revoked"    upvote was removed
      "best_answer"       answer marked best
      "doubt_solved"      answer marked as solving doubt
      "daily_submission"  daily writing answer submitted
      "study"             silent library session ended
      "mock_test"         mock test completed
